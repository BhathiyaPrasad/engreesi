# advise

🗣️ /ədˈvaɪz/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අවවාද කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

- She advised her friend about the dangers of smoking.


### ~ someone to do something

- I advise my students to study hard for their exams.


### ~ doing something

- I advise studying every day to improve your English skills.


### ~ on/about

- I always advise my students on proper grammar usage in their writing.
