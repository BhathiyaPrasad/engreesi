# anticipate

🗣️ /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### කලින් සිතා යමක් කරනවා
### අපේක්ෂා කරනවා
### බලාපොරොත්තු වෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- I anticipate success in my future endeavors.


### ~ (something) doing something

- I anticipate finishing my homework by 9 pm.


### ~ that...

- I anticipate that the weather will be nice tomorrow.


### ~ wh-

- I anticipate what the future holds.
