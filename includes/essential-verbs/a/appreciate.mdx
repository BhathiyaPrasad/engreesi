# appreciate

🗣️ /əˈpriːʃieɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අගය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

- I appreciate your hard work.


### ~ doing something

- I appreciate finishing my homework before dinner.


### ~ someone doing something

- I appreciate you helping me with this project.


### ~ that...

- I appreciate that you always come to class prepared and ready to learn.
