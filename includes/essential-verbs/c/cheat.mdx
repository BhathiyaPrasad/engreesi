# cheat

🗣️ /tʃiːt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### රවටනවා
### හොර කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

- She cheated her classmates by stealing their answers during the test.


### ~ at something

- She was caught cheating at the math test.


### ~ (on someone)

- She cheated on her boyfriend with his best friend.
