# combine

🗣️ /kəmˈbaɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### එකට එකතු කරනවා/වෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ with something

- He always combines honey with his tea in the morning.


### ~ something (with something)

- You can combine the flour with the sugar to make the dough for the cake.
