# communicate

🗣️ /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සන්නිවේදනය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ with someone/something

- I ask for a director to communicate with the shareholders.


### ~ something (to someone)

- She communicated her ideas to her team through a PowerPoint presentation.
