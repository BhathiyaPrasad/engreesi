# concentrate

🗣️ /ˈkɒnsntreɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අවධානය යොමු කරනවා
### ඒකරාශී කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (on something)

- The student concentrated on his studies and managed to ace the exam. 


### ~ something

- She concentrated her efforts on improving her grades.
