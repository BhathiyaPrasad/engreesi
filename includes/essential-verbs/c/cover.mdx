# cover

🗣️ /ˈkʌvə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ආවරණය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something (with something) (from something)

- She asked for help covering her car from the heavy rain with a tarp.


### ~ for someone

- I often have to cover for my colleagues when they take time off work.
