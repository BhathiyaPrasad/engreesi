# decrease

🗣️ /dɪˈkriːs/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ප්‍රමාණයෙන් අඩු වෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- He asks for a decrease in his workload.


### ~ (from something) (to something)

- The amount of sugar in the recipe decreased from 2 cups to 1 cup.


### ~ by

- The temperature will decrease by 5 degrees tomorrow.
