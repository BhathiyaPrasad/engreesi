# define

🗣️ /dɪˈfaɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අර්ථ දක්වනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (as something)

- "I would define myself as a hardworking individual who always strives for success."


### ~ wh-

- She asked for someone to define what the abbreviation 'wh-' stands for.
