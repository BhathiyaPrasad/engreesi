# demand

🗣️ /dɪˈmɑːnd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### තරයේ ඉල්ලා සිටිනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- He demanded a refund for the faulty product.


### ~ to do something

- He demanded to speak to the manager immediately.


### ~ that...

- I demand that my students hand in their homework on time.
