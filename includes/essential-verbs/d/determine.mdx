# determine

🗣️ /dɪˈtɜːmɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### නිවැරදිව තහවුරු කරගන්නවා
### තීරණය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- She determined the answer to the math problem using her calculator.


### ~ that...

- The jury will determine that the defendant is guilty based on the evidence presented.


### ~ wh-

- She determined what she wanted for her birthday before asking her parents to buy it.
