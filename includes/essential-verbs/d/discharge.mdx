# discharge

🗣️ /dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### මුදා හරිනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (from something)

- The doctor discharged the patient from the hospital.


### ~ something

- He asked for a glass of water to help him discharge the pills he had just swallowed.
