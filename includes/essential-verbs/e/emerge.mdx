# emerge

🗣️ /iˈmɜːdʒ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### මතු වෙනවා
### එළියට එනවා

## Usage

### ~ as something

- Sinhala requested assistance, but English emerged as the primary language of communication.


### ~ from something

- The butterfly emerged from its cocoon.


### ~ into something

- The sun slowly emerged into a bright, clear sky.
