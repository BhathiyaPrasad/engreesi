# experiment

🗣️ /ɪkˈsperɪmənt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අත්හදා බැලීම්/පරීක්ෂණ කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (on someone/something)

- I asked for permission to experiment on the lab mice.


### ~ with something

- He asked for permission to experiment with the new equipment.
