# explain

🗣️ /ɪkˈspleɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### කරුණු පැහැදිලි කරදෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ (something) (to someone)

- "Can you explain the directions to me?"


### ~ that...

- "Explain that concept to me again."



### ~ wh-

- I will explain what happened yesterday.
