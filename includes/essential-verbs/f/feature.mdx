# feature

🗣️ /ˈfiːtʃə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### විදහා දක්වනවා
### (නිර්මාණයක) පෙනී සිටිනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something (as someone/something)

- She features her new cat as the star of her Instagram page.


### ~ (in something)

- The new car model features in the latest magazine issue.
