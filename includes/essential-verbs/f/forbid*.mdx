# forbid*

🗣️ /fəˈbɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### තහනම් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (someone) something

- The teacher forbade the student sweets before dinner.


### ~ someone (to do something)

- The teacher forbade her students to use their phones during class.


### ~ someone (from doing something)

- The teacher forbade her students from using their phones during class.
