# hear*

🗣️ /hɪə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ඇහෙනවා
### ඇහුම්කන් දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

- I often hear my students ask for clarification when learning new grammar rules.


### ~ (that)

- "I heard that they're asking for volunteers for the charity event." 


### ~ wh-

- I often hear students ask for help with pronunciation.
