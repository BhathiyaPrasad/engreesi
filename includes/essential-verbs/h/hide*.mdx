# hide*

🗣️ /haɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### හැංගෙනවා
### හංගනවා

## Usage

### ~ (someone/something) (from something)

- I hid the cake from my sister in the pantry.


### ~ (someone/something) (adv./prep.)

- The child asked for a cookie but his mother hid them in the top cabinet.
