# increase

🗣️ /ɪnˈkriːs/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වැඩි වෙනවා/කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (something) (from A) (to B)

- "I asked for an increase in my salary from $50,000 to $60,000."


### ~ (something) (in/by/with something)

- "He asked for an increase in his salary by $500."
