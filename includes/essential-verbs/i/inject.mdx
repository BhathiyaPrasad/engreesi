# inject

🗣️ /ɪnˈdʒekt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### එන්නත් කරනවා
### ඇතුළට විදිනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (into someone/something)

- She asked for permission to inject the medicine into her mother's arm.


### ~ A (with B)

- He asked for a pen to be injected with blue ink for his drawing.


### ~ B (into A)

- She asked for a pen from her friend.
