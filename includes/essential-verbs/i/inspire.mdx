# inspire

🗣️ /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### නිර්මාණාත්මක කාර්යයකට ප්‍රබෝධ කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone ( with something)

- She asked for inspiration with her creative writing assignment.


### ~ someone (in something)

- She asked for my help in solving the math problem.


### ~ someone (to do something)

- I inspired my students to study harder by sharing my personal success story.
