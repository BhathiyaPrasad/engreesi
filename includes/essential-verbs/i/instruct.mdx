# instruct

🗣️ /ɪnˈstrʌkt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### උපදෙස් දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (in something)

- She instructed her students in English grammar.


### ~ someone to do something

- I will instruct you to complete the assignment before tomorrow.


### ~ that...

- I will instruct that all students arrive on time.
