# intend

🗣️ /ɪnˈtend/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අදහස් කරනවා
### අපේක්‍ෂා කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (someone/something) (to do something)

- I intend to ask for a raise at my next performance review.


### ~ doing something

- She intends asking for a raise at her performance review next week.


### ~ that...

- I intend that my students understand the concept of asking for help in class.
