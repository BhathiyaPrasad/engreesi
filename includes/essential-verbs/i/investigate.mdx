# investigate

🗣️ /ɪnˈvestɪɡeɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### විමර්ශනය කරනවා
### ප්‍රවේශමෙන් සෝදිසි කර බලනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- The detective will investigate the crime scene for any clues.


### ~ (someone) for something

- "He was investigated for fraud by the police."


### ~ wh-

- He was asked for an explanation after the authorities decided to investigate wh- happened that night.
