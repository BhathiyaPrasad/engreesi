# negotiate

🗣️ /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### එකඟතාවයකට එන්නට සාකච්ඡා කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- She is negotiating a raise with her boss.


### ~ (with someone) (for/about something)

- He successfully negotiated with his boss for a raise in salary.
