# order

🗣️ /ˈɔːdə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### නියෝග කරනවා
### ඇණවුම් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone to do something

- He ordered me to clean the dishes.


### ~ that...

- I often order that my students complete their homework before class.


### ~ someone something

- "She ordered me lunch at the restaurant."


### ~ something (for someone)

- She always orders a coffee for her colleague during their morning break.
