# persuade

🗣️ /pəˈsweɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### කෙනෙක්ව
(යමක් කරන්නට) පොළඹවා ගන්නවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (to do something)

- He managed to persuade her to go on stage and sing.


### ~ someone (into doing something)

- I tried to persuade her into coming to the party with us.
