# praise

🗣️ /preɪz/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වර්ණනා කරනවා
### ප්‍රශංසා කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something (for something)

- The teacher praised her students for their hard work.


### ~ someone/something (for doing something)

- She praised him for his dedication to his studies. 


### ~ someone/something (as something)

- He praised the new employee as a hard-working and dedicated team member.
