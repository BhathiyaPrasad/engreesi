# prefer

🗣️ /prɪˈfɜː(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වැඩිපුර කැමතියි

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

-  I prefer chocolate over vanilla ice cream.


### ~ someone/something + adj.

- He always prefers spicy food.


### ~ to do something

- I prefer to do my homework before dinner.


### ~ doing something

- The students prefer reading books instead of watching movies.


### ~ that...

- "I prefer that you come to my house for dinner."


### ~ A to B

- I prefer tea to coffee.
