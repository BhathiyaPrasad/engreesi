# presume

🗣️ /prɪˈzjuːm/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සත්‍ය යැයි සිතනවා
### අනුමාන කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (that)

- I presume that you will arrive on time.


### ~ something

- I often presume something is wrong when my students arrive late to class.


### ~ someone/something + adj.

- He presumed me guilty before the trial even began.


### ~ someone/something to be someone/something

- I presume the new student to be from Sri Lanka.


### ~ someone/something to have done something

- She presumed her friend to have finished the project on time.
