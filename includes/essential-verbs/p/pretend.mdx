# pretend

🗣️ /prɪˈtend/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### යම් දෙයක් බොරුවට අඟවනවා

## Usage

### ~ (to someone) (that)

- "She pretended to her friend that she was sick to get out of going to the party."


### ~ to be/do/have something

- She pretended to have a fever to skip school.


### ~ something

- I will pretend to be a doctor during the school play.
