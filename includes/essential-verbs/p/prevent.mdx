# prevent

🗣️ /prɪˈvent/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වළක්වනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something (doing something)

- 
She always prevents her children from eating too much sugar.


### ~ someone/something (from doing something)

- The sign outside the building prevents people from smoking inside.
