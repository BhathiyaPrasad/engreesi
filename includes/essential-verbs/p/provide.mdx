# provide

🗣️ /prəˈvaɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සපයනවා
### සලසනවා
### ලබා දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (to/for someone)

- Please provide a pencil for the student in the back. 


### ~ someone with something

- The teacher provided her students with study materials for their upcoming exam.
