# record

🗣️ /rɪˈkɔːd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වාර්තාගත කරනවා
### සටහන් කරනවා
### පටිගත කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- I asked for your record yesterday.


### ~ that...

- Record that he asked for a glass of water.


### ~ wh-

- My students often record what I am saying during class so they can listen to the audio later and review
