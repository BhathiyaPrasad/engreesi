# refer

🗣️ /rɪˈfɜː(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ගැන කතා කරනවා
### සඳහන් කරනවා
### යොමු කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ to someone/something

- She always refers to her mother for advice.


### ~ someone/something to someone/something

- I will refer my student's essay to the teacher for feedback.
