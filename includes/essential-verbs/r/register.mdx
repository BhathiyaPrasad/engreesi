# register

🗣️ /ˈredʒɪstə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ලියාපදිංචි කරනවා
### ලේඛනගත කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (in something)

- He asked to register his name in the English class roster.


### ~ (someone) as something

- I will register him as a student in our class.


### ~ (something)

- She registered her car with the DMV last week.
