# remain

🗣️ /rɪˈmeɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ඉතිරි වෙනවා
### රැඳී සිටිනවා
### පවතිනවා

## Usage

### ~ +adj./noun

- She remained silent throughout the meeting.


### ~ adv./prep.

- He remains at home every day.


### ~ (to do something)

- She remains to study for her final exams.
