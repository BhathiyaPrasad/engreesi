# remind

🗣️ /rɪˈmaɪnd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### මතක් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (about /of something)

- I reminded my brother of his dentist appointment.


### ~ someone (to do something)

- He reminded me to turn off the lights before we left the room.


### ~ someone (that)

- I will remind the students that their homework is due tomorrow.


### ~ someone (wh-)

- She asked for a reminder about the meeting tomorrow.
