# repeat

🗣️ /rɪˈpiːt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### නැවත කියනවා
### නැවත කරනවා
### නැවත නැවත සිදුවෙනවා etc.

## Usage

### ~ something (to someone)

- John asked for the teacher to repeat the instructions.


### ~ something (after someone)

- "Please repeat after me: 'I want to ask for a second chance'."


### ~ that...

- "Could you please repeat that question for me?"


### ~ what

- I asked my students to repeat what I said to make sure they understood the instructions.
