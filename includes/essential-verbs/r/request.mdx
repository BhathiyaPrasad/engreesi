# request

🗣️ /rɪˈkwest/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ඉල්ලනවා
### අයදිනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (from someone)

- The student requested a pen from their teacher.


### ~ someone to do something

-  I request my students to complete their homework on time.


### ~ that...

- The customer requested that their order be delivered by noon.
