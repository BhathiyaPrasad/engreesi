# resign

🗣️ /rɪˈzaɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ඉල්ලා අස්වෙනවා
### පදවිය අත් හරිනවා

## Usage

### ~ (from something)

- I will resign from my job tomorrow.


### ~ (as something)

- "I have decided to resign as the team captain."


### ~ something

- I asked for a raise last month, but instead my boss asked me to resign.
