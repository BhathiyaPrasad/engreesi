# restrict

🗣️ /rɪˈstrɪkt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සීමා කරනවා
### සීමාවලට යටත් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (to something)

- She restricts her children's screen time to one hour per day.


### ~ something (to someone)

- She restricts her children to only watching one hour of TV per day.
