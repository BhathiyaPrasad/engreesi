# separate

🗣️ /ˈsepəreɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වෙන් කරනවා
### වෙන් වෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ (something) (from something)

- She always separates her clothes from her husband's in the laundry.


### ~ (something) into something

- She asked for the cake to be separated into individual slices for the party.


### ~ something and something

- I asked for separate books and notebooks for each subject.
