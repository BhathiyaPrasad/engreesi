# supply

🗣️ /səˈplaɪ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සපයනවා
### ලබා දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (to someone/something)

- I ask for a raise every year during my performance review.

- The teacher supplied me with all the necessary materials for the project.
