# train

🗣️ /treɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### පුහුණු වෙනවා/කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (someone/something) (to do something)

- I am training my dog to fetch the newspaper every morning.


### ~ (someone) (as/in/for something)

- I will train you for your new job as a computer programmer.
