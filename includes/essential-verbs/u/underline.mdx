# underline

🗣️ /ˌʌndəˈlaɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### යටින් ඉරක් අඳිනවා
### අවධාරණය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- The student asked for a pencil to underline the key words in the passage.


### ~ that...

- "Can you please underline that important sentence for me?"


### ~ wh-

- He asked for me to underline who the culprit was.
