## Usage

### ~ (that)

- I wish that my students could ask for help more often during class.


### ~ someone/something + adj.

- She wishes her brother good luck on his exam.


### ~ someone/something + adv./prep.

- He wished for a peaceful resolution to the conflict.


### ~ to do something

- She wishes to go to the park.


### ~ someone something

- I wish my friend good luck on his exam tomorrow.


### ~ someone/something to do something

- I wish my students to ask for help when they need it.


### ~ for something

- She wished for a new bicycle for her birthday.
