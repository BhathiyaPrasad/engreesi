## unite

🗣️ /juˈnaɪt/

### ඒකාබද්ධ වෙනවා/කරනවා, එක්සත් වෙනවා/කරනවා

### How to use "unite" in a sentence:
#### unite in something

- Students, please unite in the common goal of creating a more sustainable community.

#### unite in doing something

- The students must unite in practicing their English skills every day.


#### unite (behind/against someone/something)

- The protesters united against the government's decision to raise taxes.

#### unite (someone/something) (with someone/something)

- The newly elected leader promised to unite the divided country with peace and prosperity.
