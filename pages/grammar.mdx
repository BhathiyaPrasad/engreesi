## Why Grammar Matters

Grammar is the foundation of effective communication. It's the set of rules and structures that govern how we organize words and phrases to convey meaning accurately. From crafting compelling essays to delivering impactful speeches, a solid grasp of grammar empowers you to express yourself clearly and persuasively.
