# decide

🗣️ /dɪˈsaɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### තීරණය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- She decided to go to the beach for the day.



### ~ between A and B

- She must decide between the red dress and the blue dress for the party.


### ~ that...

- I decided that I wanted to go to the beach tomorrow.


### ~ wh-

- She decided what to order from the menu.
