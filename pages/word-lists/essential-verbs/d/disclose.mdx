# disclose

🗣️ /dɪsˈkləʊz/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### එළිදරව් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (to someone)

- I will disclose the information to my boss.


### ~ that...

- She disclosed that she had been hiding her true feelings.


### ~ wh-

- I will disclose what happened at the party.
