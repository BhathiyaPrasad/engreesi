# distinguish

🗣️ /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වෙන්කොට හඳුනාගන්නවා

## Usage

### ~ A from B

- She can distinguish her twin sister from her by looking at their eyes.


### ~ between A and B

- "Please distinguish between the cat and the dog based on their different fur colors."
