# divide

🗣️ /dɪˈvaɪd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### කොටස් කරනවා
### බෙදා දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (into something)

- I will divide the cake into equal pieces for everyone to share.


### ~ something between A and B

- I asked for the assignment to be divided between him and her.
