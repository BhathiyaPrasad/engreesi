# drape

🗣️ /dreɪp/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ලිහිල්ව ඔතනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something in something

- The designer draped the model in a gorgeous silk gown for the red carpet event.


### ~ something around someone/something

- She draped the blanket around her shoulders to keep warm.
