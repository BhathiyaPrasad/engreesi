# engage

🗣️ /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### යමකට ඈඳාගන්නවා/බැඳගන්නවා
### වැඩක යෙදෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- The students were asked to engage their imaginations during the creative writing activity.


### ~ someone/something to do something

- She engages the tutor to help her study for her exams.


### ~ with someone

- I often engage with my students after class to check on their understanding of the lesson.
