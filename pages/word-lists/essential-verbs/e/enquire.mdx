# enquire

🗣️ /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### පුදගලයෙක්ගෙන් යමක් විමසනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- "The student enquired the price of the book."


### ~ about someone/something

- She enquired about the price of the dress.


### ~ wh-

- She enquired when the next bus would arrive.
