# establish

🗣️ /ɪˈstæblɪʃ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### පිහිටුවනවා
### ස්ථාපනය කරනවා
### තහවුරු කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- The company had to establish its reputation before expanding into new markets.


### ~ someone/something (in something) (as something)

- He established his company in the market as a reliable and trustworthy brand.
