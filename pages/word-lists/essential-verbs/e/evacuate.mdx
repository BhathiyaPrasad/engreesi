# evacuate

🗣️ /ɪˈvækjueɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### (මිනිසුන්ගෙන්) හිස් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- The government ordered residents to evacuate their homes due to the approaching hurricane.


### ~ someone (from …) (to …)

- The government decided to evacuate the residents from the flooded town to a nearby shelter.
