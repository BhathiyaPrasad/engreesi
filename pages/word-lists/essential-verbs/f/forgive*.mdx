# forgive*

🗣️ /fəˈɡɪv/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වරදට සමාව දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (for something)

- She forgave her friend for forgetting her birthday. 


### ~ someone (for doing something)

- I forgave her for forgetting my birthday.


### ~ someone something

- She forgave him his mistakes.
