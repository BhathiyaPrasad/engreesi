# include

🗣️ /ɪnˈkluːd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අඩංගු කරනවා
### අන්තර්ගත කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- She always includes dessert in her dinner plans.


### ~ doing something

- I often ask for extra time on assignments.


### ~ someone/something (as/in/on something)

- One way you can use the verb phrase 'include someone/something (as/in/on something)'
