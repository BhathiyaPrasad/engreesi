# indicate

🗣️ /ˈɪndɪkeɪt/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### පෙන්නුම් කරනවා
### අඟවනවා

## Usage

### ~ (something) (to someone)

- I will indicate the correct answer to you.



### ~ that...

- The student's raised hand indicated that they had a question.


### ~ wh-

- Please indicate which book you would like to ask for.
