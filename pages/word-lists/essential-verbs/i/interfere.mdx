# interfere

🗣️ /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### බාධා කරනවා
### අනුන්ගේ කටයුතුවලට අතපොවනවා

## Usage

### ~ (in something)

- The manager asked for her opinions but she didn't want to interfere in their discussion.


### ~ (with someone/something)

- I asked for his help with the project.
