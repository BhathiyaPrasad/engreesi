# introduce

🗣️ /ˌɪntrəˈdjuːs/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### හඳුන්වා දෙනවා
### ඉදිරිපත් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something (to someone)

- "I will introduce you to my best friend at the party tonight."


### ~ someone/something (as someone/something)

- She always introduces herself as a doctor at parties.
