# mark

🗣️ /mɑːk/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### සලකුණු කරනවා
### ලකුණක් යොදනවා
### ලකුණු දෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ A (with B)

- She always marks her calendar with upcoming events.


### ~ B on A

- Please mark B on A.


### ~ someone/something as something

- She marked him as the winner of the competition.
