# outline

🗣️ /ˈaʊtlaɪn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### අදාළ කරුණු සැකිල්ලක් සේ දක්වනවා

## Usage

### ~ something (to someone)

- She asked for a clear outline of the project to be explained to her.


### ~ wh-

- She asked for her professor to outline why the assignment was due on a different date than originally planned
