# prescribe

🗣️ /prɪˈskraɪb/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### (පොතක්/බෙහෙතක්) නියම කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ something

- "I prescribed her some antibiotics for her sore throat."


### ~ (someone) something (for something)

- The doctor will prescribe the patient medicine for their cold.


### ~ that...

- I prescribe that you take this medicine twice a day.


### ~ wh-

- She prescribed what my father needed for his illness.
