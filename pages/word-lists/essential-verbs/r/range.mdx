# range

🗣️ /reɪndʒ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### පරාසයක පවතිනවා

## Usage

### ~ from A to B

- "The prices of our products range from $10 to $50."


### ~ between A and B

- The temperatures this week range between 20 and 25 degrees Celsius.


### ~ adv./prep.

- She asked for a raise at work.
