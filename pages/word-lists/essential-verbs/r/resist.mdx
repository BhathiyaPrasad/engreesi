# resist

🗣️ /rɪˈzɪst/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### විරෝධය පානවා
### අවනත වීම ප්‍රතික්ෂේප කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (something)

- He tried to resist the temptation but ultimately gave in.


### ~ doing something

- He couldn't resist asking for more ice cream even though he had already finished his cone.
