# respond

🗣️ /rɪˈspɒnd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### ප්‍රතිචාර දක්වනවා

## Usage

### ~ (to someone/something) (with something)

- The students asked for more time to complete their homework and I responded with an extra day.


### ~ that...

- "When I asked for help, she responded that she was too busy to assist me."


### ~ (to someone/something) (by doing something)

- "When the teacher asked for volunteers, she responded by raising her hand."
