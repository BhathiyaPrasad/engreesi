# retire

🗣️ /rɪˈtaɪə(r)/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### විශ්‍රාම ගන්නවා/ගන්වනවා

## Usage

### ~ (from something)

- She is planning to retire from her job as a doctor next year.


### ~ (to something)

- She retired to her family's farmhouse in the countryside.


### ~ someone

- I will retire my father next year.
