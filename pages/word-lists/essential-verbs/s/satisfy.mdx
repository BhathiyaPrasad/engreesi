# satisfy

🗣️ /ˈsætɪsfaɪ/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### තෘප්තිමත් කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (of someone)

- Tom satisfied his boss of his hard work.


### ~ something

- He always asks for more food because he can never satisfy his hunger.


### ~ someone (that)

- I will satisfy my boss that I am the best candidate for the job.
