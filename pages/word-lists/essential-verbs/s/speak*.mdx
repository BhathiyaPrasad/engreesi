# speak*

🗣️ /spiːk/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### කතා කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ (to/with someone) (about something/someone)

- She spoke to her boss about the new project.


### ~ of someone/something

- She always speaks highly of her children's achievements.


### ~ adv./prep.

- I always speak to my grandparents in Sinhala.
