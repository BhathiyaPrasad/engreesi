# surround

🗣️ /səˈraʊnd/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### වට කරනවා
### වටේට තියෙනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone/something

- The soldiers surrounded the enemy stronghold.


### ~ someone/something (with someone/something) [P.V.]

- The tourists surrounded the famous monument with their cameras and selfie sticks.
