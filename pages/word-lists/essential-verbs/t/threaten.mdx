# threaten

🗣️ /ˈθretn/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### තර්ජනය කරනවා

## Usage

### ~ someone (with something)

- He threatened her with a knife.


### ~ (someone ) that

- "She threatened her boyfriend that she would leave him if he didn't stop ignoring her."


### ~ to do something

- She threatened to call the police if he didn't stop harassing her.
