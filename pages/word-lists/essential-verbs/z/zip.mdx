# zip

🗣️ /zɪp/

## Sinhala Meaning(s)

### zip කරනවා (අරිනවා/වහනවා)

## Usage

### ~ something

- She asked for me to zip her dress.


### ~ someone into something

- I asked for a blanket, but instead, they zipped me into a sleeping bag.


### ~ something + adj.

- She zipped her jacket tight against the cold wind.


### ~ (someone/something) (up/together)

- She zipped up her coat before heading outside in the cold.
